About Us

We are a team of innovative chemical and materials engineering students dedicated to providing non-toxic and biodegradable flocculants for companies to achieve efficient and eco-friendly flocculation. Established in 2024, AquaFloc drives innovation in environmental conservation by creating cutting-edge solutions for industrial wastewater treatment.

Our mission is to support industries in achieving sustainable water management practices, helping them reduce their environmental impact while maintaining high standards of efficiency.

Services and Products

Chemical Agents for Wastewater Treatment: AquaFloc specializes in the development of chemical agents designed to treat wastewater, with a focus on industries such as petrochemicals, textiles, and manufacturing. Our agents enhance contaminant breakdown, ensuring cleaner effluents and improved compliance with environmental standards.

Enhanced Performance: Our treatments are both highly effective and sustainable, eliminating pollutants without causing secondary pollution. Being biodegradable, our flocculants minimize the risk of accumulating in the ecosystem, providing an environmentally safe solution for industries.
